Taptator
Challenge your neurons

Taptator is an innovative, fast paced and highly addictive game that is simple to learn but hard to master. It challenges the speed at which your brain processes visual and textual information, makes logical decisions and sends motor signals to your muscles.

How to play?

Tap on the right side if the description matches the displayed shape.
Tap on the left side if the description doesn't match the displayed shape.
